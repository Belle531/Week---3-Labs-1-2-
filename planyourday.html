Preparationis the key to Success:

Phase 1 Days (1-3)

  • Goal: Ease your body into the juice fast, reducing cravings and preparing your digestive system.
  • Food: Gradually reduce solid food intake, focusing on fruits, vegetables, and light meals. Increase water intake. Eliminate processed foods, sugar, caffeine, and alcohol.
  • Mindset: Reflect on your intentions for the fast. What do you hope to achieve? Starting Journaling and practicing mindfulness.
  • Tips: Start prepping your kitchen - clean out unhealthy foods and stock up on fresh produce.

Important Note: Consult with your healthcare provider before starting any juice fast, especially if you have any underlying health conditions. This plan is a guide and may need to be adjusted based on your individual needs. This information is not intended to replace medical advice.

Getting Started Juicing 101

Refreshing Pineapple Ginger Turmeric Drink Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add ice cubes if desired and blend again.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
