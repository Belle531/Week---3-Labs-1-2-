Here's a Quick Look In Preparation:

Phase 1: (Days 1-3)

  • Goal: Ease your body into the juice fast, reducing craving and preparing your digestive system.
  • Food: Gradually reduce solid food intake, focusing on fruits, vegetables, and light meanls. Increase water intake. Eliminate processed foods, sugar, caffeine, and alcohol.
  • Mindset: Reflect on your intentions for the fast. What do you hope to achieve? Starting Journaling and practicing mindfulness.
  • Tips: Start prepping you kitchen - clean out unhealthy foods and stock up on fresh produce.

Important Note: Consult with your healthcare provider before Starting any juice fast, especially if you have any underlying health conditions. This plan is a guide and may need to be adjusted based on your individual needs. This information is not to intended to replace medical advice.
