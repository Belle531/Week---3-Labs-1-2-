Our meeting place will be on Google Meet. Please check our Slack channel for the Google Meet code or link to Join To ensure a productive and respectful learning environment for everyone, I've out lined a few guidelines for our study sessions:


Study Session Tips & Tricks:

It's Rubber Duck Time

  • Preparation is Key: Please come prepared with your class materials, computer ready,and any questions you have writeen down beforehand. This will help us maximize out time together and ensure everyone gets their questions answered
  • Respect Communication: To avoid interrupting someone's train of thought, please raise your hand virtually or send a message depending on the platform we use before speaking. Let's be mindful of others and allow everyone a chance to get the help they need.
  • Mute When Not Speakeing: Please keep your microphone muted unless you are actively speaking. This will help minimize background noise and distractions, allowing everyone to focus.
  • Teamwork Makes the Dream Work: Remember,we are teammates on theis jorurney! We're here to support and encourage one another

We look forward to learning and growing together. Let's make the most out of our study sessions!
